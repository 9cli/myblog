以下是关于“抛弃”相关单词的用法辨析，点击标题即可展开。

<details>
  <summary style="cursor: pointer; font-weight: bold; padding: 10px; background: #f8f9fa; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-radius: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px;">
    1. Abandon (彻底遗弃)
  </summary>
  <div style="padding: 15px; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-top: none; margin-bottom: 15px;">
    
  - **核心：** 永久性地离开或放弃。
  - **对象：** 人、船只、计划、希望。
  - **例句：** The sailors were forced to **abandon** the ship.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="cursor: pointer; font-weight: bold; padding: 10px; background: #f8f9fa; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-radius: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px;">
    2. Desert (背弃职责)
  </summary>
  <div style="padding: 15px; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-top: none; margin-bottom: 15px;">
    
  - **核心：** 违背义务、契约或在危难时刻离开。
  - **对象：** 军队、岗位、家庭。
  - **例句：** He **deserted** his family when they needed him most.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary style="cursor: pointer; font-weight: bold; padding: 10px; background: #f8f9fa; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-radius: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px;">
    3. Discard (丢弃无用物)
  </summary>
  <div style="padding: 15px; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-top: none; margin-bottom: 15px;">
    
  - **核心：** 认为某物多余或无用而将其扔掉。
  - **对象：** 垃圾、旧物、过时的观念。
  - **例句：** Please **discard** any old food from the fridge.
  </div>
</details>
