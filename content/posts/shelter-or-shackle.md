### Shelter or Shackle?

We build walls to carve out a sanctuary, seeking safety in the boundaries we draw. Yet, the same stones that shelter us from the storm eventually block out the light. 

We set out to build a refuge, only to find we’ve built a fortress—and the tragedy of a fortress is that while nothing gets in, the soul is often the first prisoner.
